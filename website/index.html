Project Overview

Our autonomous robot is engineered for high-precision navigation, specifically designed for a target-touching challenge. Integrating mechanical innovation, robust electronics, and intelligent code, the bot operates independently to reach and accurately contact the target zone. This documentation captures objectives, domain-specific teamwork, and the collaborative process behind our solution.
